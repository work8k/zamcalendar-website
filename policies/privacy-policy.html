Privacy Policy

Who We Are?

"Dama Code For Information Technology" is a company registered in Saudi Arabia, authenticated by the Saudi Business Center under Authentication Number #0000155228. We develop the "ZAM Calendar" App to help users manage their work schedule efficiently.
This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect from our customers, how we use it, and the choices available to them regarding its use. It also explains how customers can contact us with any questions or concerns about our practices.

No Need for any Registration and Sign In
Our app is designed for use without requiring sign-up, registration, or sign-in. You do not need to provide your name or email, and we do not collect this information.

Total Privacy We Never Access Your Personal Event Data
Your Personal Event data (means the calendar events you create) remains private and accessible only by you. It saved on your device (or your iCloud account, if enabled). We never have access to your event data. We never upload or save your event data on our servers.

- Local Storage: All the personal event data you enter stays securely on your device.
- iCloud Storage: If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization, your personal event data is stored in your personal iCloud account. This process is handled solely by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

We do not collect any data that directly identifies you
The information you provide below is completely anonymous and cannot be linked to your identity. It is used solely to ensure that the app functions as intended. This includes:

- Country: defined as the country you select, is used to provide you with a personalized national holidays calendar, featuring public holidays specific to your country.
- Company Name: defined as the company you select, is used to generate a holiday calendar event that reflects your organization's holiday schedule.
- Shift Schedule: defined a the shifts you enter. Used for for analytical purposes, helping us understand usage patterns and improve the service.
- Calendar Image: If you choose to share your calendar image, we will use it only to populate your in-app calendar. This image is not shared with any third party and is used solely for app functionality.

All the information stays anonymous and is only used for the app's functionality.

Usage Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to track which pages you visit in our app (for example, the Month Page or Year Page). No personal data is collected. Only anonymous page view data is gathered to help improve the app.

Syncing and Backup
You can use the app completely on your device without having to sign in or sign up. If you want your data to be backed up or synced across your iOS devices, you can enable iCloud synchronization. When you do this, your data is transferred to and stored in your personal iCloud account. This process is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud service and follows Apple's privacy policies.

Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your data:

- Right to Be Informed: You can learn how we collect, use, and store your data.
- Right of Access: You can ask to see the data you have provided.
- Right to Correct: If something is wrong with your data, you can ask us to fix it.
- Right to Delete: You may ask us to delete your data when appropriate.
- Right to Withdraw Consent: You can stop us from processing your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Complaint or Objection Filing
If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us.

Updates
We may update this privacy policy at any time. Your rights will not be reduced without your clear consent. Any changes will be posted on this page. Check this policy regularly to stay informed.

By using the "ZAM Calendar" app developed by "Dama Code For Information Technology", you agree that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined.
